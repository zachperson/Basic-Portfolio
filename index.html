Zach Person

About Me

Zach Person is an American singer/songwriter and guitarist. Growing up in North Carolina, he was exposed to music through his family and influental members of the community. He began playing guitar and sining at the age of 10. His family relocated to Houston just before he turned 13. It was here in the state of Texas where he began performing live shows with the band Project X. Eventually, Zach moved on to pursue a solo career. Throughout high-school, he pefromed a solo artist around the Houston area. Shortly after graduating from George Bush High School, he went on to compete on Season 15 of American Idol, making it into the top 24 guys group. Most recently, he relaocated to Austin, TX in August of 2017. He is currently working on releasing a collection of new music for the summer of 2018.
